# Chronometer — Support

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I add a country to favorites?
Open the World Clock tab, select a country, then tap the star icon. You can also swipe right on any country in the country picker.

### How do I remove a favorite?
Long-press a favorite card and select "Remove from Favorites", or swipe left on the country in the picker.

### How do I use the interactive globe?
Drag or swipe on the 3D globe to rotate it. Select a country to focus the globe on that location. The globe supports full touch interaction on both iPhone and iPad.

### The globe is not loading
The interactive globe requires an internet connection on first launch to load. Ensure you're connected to Wi-Fi or cellular data.

### How do I find the best meeting time?
Add 2 or more favorites, then open the Planner tab. It automatically calculates the best overlapping business hours.

### What are business hours?
The app uses standard business hours (9 AM - 5 PM, Monday-Friday) in each country's local time zone.

### What is Golden Hour?
Golden hour is the period shortly after sunrise and before sunset when sunlight is soft and warm - ideal for photography.

### How do I convert time between countries?
Open the Converter tab, select two countries, and drag the time slider to compare times side by side.
