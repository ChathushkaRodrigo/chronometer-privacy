Privacy Policy for Chronometer

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Overview

Chronometer ("the App") is developed by Chathushka Rodrigo. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information the App collects, stores, and shares.

Data Collection

Chronometer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We have no servers, no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising.

Local Storage

The App stores your favorite countries list locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Network Requests

The App loads a lightweight open-source globe visualization library (COBE) from a CDN on first launch. No personal data is sent with this request.

Third-Party Services

Chronometer does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
